For the busy professional

Who is Pareto MBA for?

  • New or aspiring leaders

    You are a specialist in tech, research or similar who is aspiring for a leadership role. The Pareto MBA will give you the skills needed to participate in decision making processes.

  • Business owners

    You are running a small or medium-sized company but lack formal business skills. Our program will help you to avoid a lot of common pitfalls.

  • Business developers and advisors

    You want a better toolkit for analysing and improving businesses that you work with as an investor or advisor.

  • Curious professionals

    You want to grow in your role by learning about the latest business trends, marketing tactics, productivity tools and consumer behaviors.

Our curriculum

Week 1: Basic Business Skills and Model Thinking

You will learn key concepts in business, core quantitative skills and why some businesses are better than others.

Week 2: Opportunities and Strategy

You will learn how to spot business opportunites, both for starting new businesses and for developing existing ones.

Week 3: Marketing and Positioning

You will learn how to approach the market with a new product or service, using the latest tools and techniques.

Week 4: Growth, Expansion and Finance

You will learn how to grow a business, and how to finance the growth.

Week 5: Management and Operations

You will learn how to lead yourself and your team, and how to manage and optimize operations.

Week 6: Dealing with Adversity

You will learn about how to manage yourself and your company when times get tough.

Week 7: Business Developement

You will learn how to look for and identify opportunities for improvement throughout a business.

Week 8: Mergers, Acquisitions and Synergies

You will learn the basiscs of buying and selling companies.
