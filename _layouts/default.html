Our curriculum

We have condensed a traditional MBA curriculum into an 8-week cohort program using a flipped-classroom approach: Study each topic in your own pace during the week, and discuss it together with teachers and peers in a weekly Zoom classroom.

Week 1: Basic Business Skills and Model Thinking

You will learn key concepts in business and core quantitative skills that will be needed in the weeks to come.

Week 2: Accounting and Analytics

You will learn to read financial statements, understand what things to look for and why some companies are better business than others.

Week 3: Opportunities and Strategy

You will learn how to spot business opportunites, both for starting new businesses and for developing existing ones.

Week 4: Marketing and Positioning

You will learn how to approach the market with a new product or service, using the latest tools and techniques.

Week 5: Sales, Deals and Negotiation

You will learn how to sell and negotiate deals with customers and vendors.

Week 6: Leadership and Management

You will learn how to lead yourself and your team, and how to manage and optimize operations.

Week 7: Growth, Expansion and Finance

You will learn how to grow a business, and how to finance the growth.

Week 7: Mergers, Acquisitions and Synergies

You will learn the basiscs of buying and selling companies.
